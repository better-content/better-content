# Post-AE2 Branch Plan

Post-AE2 progression is intentionally a small number of strong branches rather than another long linear tech ladder. AE2 remains local site intelligence; these branches improve local manufacturing, bounded storage, source integration, and body-scale rewards without creating global logistics or infinite matter.

## Branches

### Quantum Manufacturing

- Starts after `ae2:spatial_io_port`.
- Uses Advanced AE quantum structure, reaction chamber, quantum core, quantum crafter, quantum accelerator, and quantum multi-threader.
- Purpose: high-throughput local pattern work and late fabrication throughput.
- Constraint: it scales a committed site, not intersite logistics.

### Extended Local Intelligence

- Uses ExtendedAE / Ex Pattern Provider machines: extended drive, extended interface, extended pattern provider, and assembler matrix.
- Purpose: make complex local factories manageable after the player has proven AE2, OC2R, space, and chemistry competence.
- Constraint: no global wireless logistics premise.

### Bounded Deep Storage

- Uses AE2 Additions super cell housing, 1024k super cells, and disk cells.
- Purpose: bigger local buffers for post-AE2 manufacturing.
- Constraint: no infinity cells or creative storage rewards.

### Source-AE Hybrid

- Uses Ars Energistique source jar/cell bridge after assembler matrix plus Ethereal Slate magic permission.
- Purpose: give late magic a real post-AE2 integration point without letting Ars replace Blood Magic tiering.
- Constraint: source is still a routed resource, not a universal replacement for logistics.

### Quantum Body Rewards

- Uses Advanced AE quantum upgrade base, lava immunity card, regeneration card, magnet card, and quantum armor.
- Purpose: reward extreme Y-band and synthesis work with body-scale capability.
- Constraint: these are high-cost milestone rewards, not early survival utilities or creative flight.

## Quest Coverage

`config/ftbquests/quests/chapters/post_ae2.snbt` now contains 16 quests covering these branches. The chapter is generated by `tools/generate_expert_quest_book.mjs`; edit the generator, not the generated SNBT, for durable changes.

## Current Limits

This pass wires the quest graph and existing recipe gates. It does not add new post-AE2 mods. Candidate future additions should be evaluated by whether they provide a complete branch that can consume quantum manufacturing, synthesis outputs, or Blood Magic/Ars hybrid materials without violating the bounded matter and bounded distance rules.
